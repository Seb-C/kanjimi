The world is yours

We are developing a browser extension that works with any Japanese website. Once installed, useful information is automatically added to the text.

Improve your vocabulary

In order to learn, a complete translation of the text is not helpful. That is why we focus on providing a translation and pronunciation for every word.

Study the words and Kanjis

Integrated dictionary

Click on a word to show a detailed description about it. The meanings are displayed separately for each possible reading of a word.

Supports multiple languages

The translations can be displayed in multiple languages, including 🇬🇧 English, 🇫🇷 French, 🇪🇸 Spanish, 🇩🇪 German and many others.

Information about the Kanjis

We will also display information about the Kanjis, including the readings and meanings.
